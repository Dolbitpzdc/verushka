My Achievements

I am a mother of the Belarusian ballroom. I've been teaching and developing Vogue culture in Belarus for more than 10 years.

RUNWAY ONLINE

This course is about European Runway - a feminine walk in one line with additional hips. In this course, you will learn how to walk, pose and represent yourself. The information is presented in the format of professionally filmed lessons (lasting from 10 to 16 minutes).

For convenience, the information is divided into programs for different levels of training: BASIC COURSE and PRO COURSE. Depending on your level and the purpose of completing the course, you can choose one or two programs at once.

Basic Course

BASIC COURSE is a program for beginners. In this version of the course, you will learn the basics of walking and posing, work with styling and pick up your look, learn how to show your clothing items and accessories. The course will be interesting for those who want to learn how to walk in heels, pose for photos and just feel more confident and feminine.

PRO Course

PRO COURSE is a program for advanced level. The PRO version of the course is for those who walk or plan to walk at vogue balls in the European Runway categories. In this version of the course, you will learn how to prepare for the ball, how to work with your outfit, how to act at your selection to the get 10's, what battle tactics you can use, and also get tips on finding individual style.
